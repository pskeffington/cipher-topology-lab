## Key caution

The project is a reproducible diagnostic and measurement study. It must not be framed as breaking AES, proving cipher security, or replacing cryptanalysis. Claims should remain limited to whether selected TDA features distinguish controlled cipher-output and weak-generator conditions under documented embeddings and backends.
